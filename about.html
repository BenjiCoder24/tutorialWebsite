About Us

Our Mission

I'm passionate about programming, teaching, and exploring. I love to teach and see how my students grow and how we can change the world. We aim to provide high-quality, accessible programming tutorials to students in the Philippines. Our tutorials are designed to cater to learners at various levels, from beginners to advanced programmers.

Who We Are

My name is Benjamin Mager, and I live in NY. I am a passionate educator and software developer dedicated to promoting technology education. Together with a team of skilled contributors and educators, we strive to offer the best learning experience.

Support Team

While we do not have formal partners, I am supported in this endeavor by my sister, who is a student, and my high school CS teacher. Both have been instrumental in developing the content and providing insights that enhance the educational value of our tutorials.
