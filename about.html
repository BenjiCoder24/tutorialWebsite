About CodeWithBenji.AI

Empowering the next generation of developers through free, accessible programming education.

Our Mission

I'm passionate about programming, teaching, and exploring. I love to teach and see how my students grow and how we can change the world.

My goal is to provide high-quality, accessible programming tutorials to students in the Philippines. My tutorials are designed to cater to learners at various levels, from beginners to advanced programmers.

Who I Am

My name is Benjamin Mager. I'm a student at UC Santa Barbara studying Economics and Computer Science.

I am a passionate educator and software developer dedicated to promoting technology education and creating opportunities for underserved communities.

Our Vision

Supporting Communities

CodeWithBenji.AI organization, in partnership with local initiatives, is raising funds to support communities in Sagunto, Panasignane, Philippines.

We focus on unemployed individuals, single mothers, and unemployed teenagers to provide them with skills and opportunities for a better future through technology education.

Our Values

Free Education

All tutorials are completely free and will remain free forever. Quality education should be accessible to everyone.

Community First

Building a supportive learning community where students help each other grow and succeed together.

Hands-On Learning

Every tutorial includes real projects and practical exercises. Learn by building, not just watching.

Global Impact

Reaching students around the world, with a special focus on communities in the Philippines.

Ready to Start Learning?

Join our community of learners and start your programming journey today. All tutorials are free and designed for all skill levels.
